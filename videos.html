UPV Miagao Campus Trip

Experience a guided visual journey around the UP Visayas Miagao Campus. This video showcases the university’s scenic landscapes, academic buildings, and student spaces, giving viewers a feel of everyday campus life and the peaceful coastal environment that defines UPV.

UPV Miagao Campus “Hagdan” CFOS to WETLAB

Follow the iconic “Hagdan” pathway connecting CFOS to the WETLAB facilities. The video highlights one of the most recognizable walks in campus life, showing the elevation changes, greenery, and the daily routes students take between classes and laboratories.

UPV New Library Tour

Take a closer look inside the UPV New Library, featuring modern study areas, learning spaces, and academic resources available to students. The tour presents the library as a central hub for research, collaboration, and quiet study within the university.
